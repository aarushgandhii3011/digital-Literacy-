# 📱 Responsible Social Media Use

## ✅ Do's
- Be respectful and professional in your posts.
- Use privacy settings to control who sees your content.
- Share accurate information and verify sources before posting.
- Build a positive digital presence that reflects your goals.
- Engage constructively in academic and professional communities.

## ❌ Don'ts
- Don’t post offensive or discriminatory content.
- Don’t overshare personal details like addresses or phone numbers.
- Don’t spread rumors or misinformation.
- Don’t engage in online arguments or cyberbullying.
- Don’t neglect your professional image by posting inappropriate material.
