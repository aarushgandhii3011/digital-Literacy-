# 🔒 Stay Safe Online Checklist

| # | Tip                                                                 | Category            |
|---|---------------------------------------------------------------------|---------------------|
| 1 | Use strong, unique passwords and enable two-factor authentication.  | General Security    |
| 2 | Keep devices updated with the latest security patches and antivirus.| General Security    |
| 3 | Avoid clicking on suspicious links or attachments in emails/SMS.    | Phishing Protection |
| 4 | Be mindful of what you share on social media; protect personal info.| Privacy             |
| 5 | Connect only to trusted Wi-Fi networks; avoid public Wi-Fi for banking.| Network Safety   |
| 6 | Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or banking credentials with anyone.  | Financial/UPI Safety|
| 7 | Verify merchant names and apps before making UPI payments.          | Financial/UPI Safety|
| 8 | Always log out of shared/public computers after use.                | Device Safety       |

## 📢 Reporting Channels
If you face online fraud, harassment, or cybercrime:  
- Report immediately at the **National Cyber Crime Portal** → [cybercrime.gov.in](https://cybercrime.gov.in)  
- Call the **Cybercrime Helpline: 1930**
